Championships 2026

📍Prague, Czech Republic 📅June 25 – July 4, 2026

Positioning

The European Box Lacrosse Championships is the highest-level box lacrosse event in Europe, bringing together the continent’s national teams in a structured championship environment. The 2026 edition in Prague is the largest to date, featuring both men’s and women’s competitions and marking a significant expansion of the European box landscape. With 19 men’s teams and 8 women’s teams competing across three venues, EBLC 2026 represents the full competitive spectrum of European box lacrosse.

2026 Snapshot

June 25 – July 4, 2026 · Prague. The third edition of the European Box Lacrosse Championships will take place across three venues (Radotín, Jižní Město, Malešice), with the introduction of a women’s competition alongside the men’s tournament. Games will be played over 10 days, with free streaming available online and full continental participation expected.

FTL Context

EBLC is the only environment where European national teams are exposed under real competitive pressure in box lacrosse. It separates structured programs from opportunistic rosters, revealing which countries have built sustainable box ecosystems — and which have not. The gap between established nations and emerging programs is most visible here. For FTL, EBLC is the clearest indicator of true national box lacrosse strength in Europe.
