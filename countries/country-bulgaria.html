01 Overview

The Bulgaria Lacrosse Scene

Bulgaria’s lacrosse programme was founded in 2007 by Nikolay Cholakov. The Bulgarian Lacrosse Federation received official recognition from the Bulgarian Olympic Committee in 2024–2025, marking a significant step toward institutional legitimacy.

Head coach Tucker LaBelle (appointed 2024) implements a strict homegrown policy: only players with genuine Bulgarian roots, with no heritage recruitment. With 4 active clubs and involvement in Balkans League planning alongside Serbia and Turkey, Bulgaria is building a programme with a long-term vision.

03 National Teams

Bulgaria on the World Stage

Men’s · 2025 EC participant

Men’s National Team

Men’s programme active under coach Tucker LaBelle. Participated in the 2025 EC (lost 0–19 to Finland in group stage). Building with full homegrown roster.

Balkans · Regional coordination

Balkans League

Bulgaria is a central partner in Balkans League development alongside Serbia, Turkey and Croatia — a regional structure designed to provide competitive depth for emerging programmes.
