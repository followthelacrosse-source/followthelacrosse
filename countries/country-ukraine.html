01 Overview

The Ukraine Lacrosse Scene

Ukraine joined the ELF in 2018 and built a developing lacrosse programme before the February 2022 Russian invasion disrupted training and displaced players across Europe. Despite the war, the national programme has persisted.

In 2025, both national teams competed at the European Championship in Wrocław. The UNDP adaptive sports training in Kyiv (March 2025) introduced lacrosse to war-affected youth, while the Athletes for Ukraine coalition has used sport as a platform for solidarity and peace.

03 National Teams

Ukraine on the World Stage

Men’s + Women’s · 2025 EC

National Teams

04 Why Follow

Why Follow Ukraine?

🏆

Resilience — competing through displacement and reconstruction

Both Ukrainian national teams competing at the 2025 EC while the country faces ongoing war is one of the most significant stories in European lacrosse.

🌐

UNDP adaptive sports programme for war-affected youth

The UNDP Kyiv programme (March 2025) uses lacrosse as a tool for recovery and inclusion for war-affected youth — a dimension of the sport rare anywhere in Europe.
