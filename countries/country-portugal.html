01 Overview

The Portugal Lacrosse Scene

Portugal joined the ELF in 2016. The Lisboa Gladiators were the first club (September 2015) and remain the driving force behind the national scene, which has grown steadily around a strong Sixes identity.

Portugal has become a significant Sixes tournament destination. The EuroLax Sixes Cup in Monte Gordo (Algarve) draws 350+ players annually, while the Lisboa Lacrosse Cup reached its 9th edition in 2025 with 16 teams from 6+ countries. The Women’s national team made their debut in 2024, and Portugal hosted 12 teams at the 2025 Women’s Sixes Qualifier for World Games qualification.

03 National Teams

Portugal on the World Stage

Men’s · National programme

Men’s National Team

Men’s programme active since ELF membership in 2016. Competes in the European Sixes circuit including the EuroLax Sixes Cup in the Algarve.

Women · Debut 2024

Women’s Programme

Women’s national team made their debut in 2024. Portugal hosted the 2025 Women’s Sixes Qualifier (March 4–9) with 12 teams competing for World Games qualification.
