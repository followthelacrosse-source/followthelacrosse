01 Overview

The Finland Lacrosse Scene

Finland is one of Nordic lacrosse's quiet infrastructure builders. Suomen Lacrosseliitto, affiliated to the European Lacrosse Federation since 2004, hosted the inaugural European Box Lacrosse Championship in 2017 in Turku with 14 nations — a defining moment for European box.

On the pitch, Finland has qualified for the 2027 World Championship Division II through the 2025 European Championship, including a 19-0 pool result against Bulgaria. Box head coach Tracey Kelusky leads the Praha26 programme, with tryouts held in Turku in September 2025 and monthly national-team training from October.

The short-term horizon is clear: the 2026 European Box Championship in Prague (24 June – 4 July) is the programme's target event.
