01 Overview

The Austria Lacrosse Scene

Austria joined the ELF in 2005. The Vienna Monarchs, founded in 2007, have been the engine of Austrian lacrosse for nearly two decades — celebrating their 20th anniversary in 2025. The federation operates through a distinctive cross-border competitive model.

Austrian lacrosse uses the Czech NBLL, the Slovak SLL, and the Donau regional circuit to provide competition. In 2025, Austria made history with its first dedicated NBLL team. The Twin Stick Bavaria partnership with VER Selb ice hockey provides summer infrastructure for box lacrosse development.
